Dewi Sintiari

Hello! Welcome to my professional webpage.

I am a junior lecturer and researcher at the Informatics Engineering Department, Computer Science Study Program, of Universitas Pendidikan Ganesha Indonesia.

Prior to that, I undertook my bachelor's degree in Mathematics Education at Universitas Pendidikan Ganesha Indonesia, a master's degree in Theoretical Computer Science at ENS de Lyon France, and I carried out my 3-year PhD projects at LIP of ENS de Lyon under the direction of Nicolas Trotignon.

I am interested in exploring some topics on Discrete Mathematics and Theoretical Computer Science, particularly Graph Theory, Combinatorics, and Algorithms. My current research focuses on problems in structural graph theory, specifically on exploring the structural and algorithmic properties of classes of graphs characterized by forbidden induced subgraphs. My curriculum vitae is available here, and links to my papers can be found in my research page.

I am currently doing postdoc in the Research Group of Discrete Mathematics in Wilfrid Laurier University with Dr. Kathie Cameron.

By the way, I also have a personal blog containing some random yet fun things I do in my spare time. If you want to check it out, you can click the "See my journal" button on the left side of this page. The writings are in Indonesian. (Currently, the site is under development.)

Happy surfing!
